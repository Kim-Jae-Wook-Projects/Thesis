# Naturalness Evaluation Survey Dataset

## Contents

The file has two sheets. `Ratings` contains 3,520 ratings from 20 raters who scored 176 input-response pairs on a 1 to 5 Likert scale. `Participants` contains the 20 anonymized raters with gender, age band, and masked email.

## Sample

Google Forms returned 36 submissions. Duplicate emails were resolved by keeping the latest submission, yielding 32 unique respondents. The final 20 are respondents within the pre-registered target age range of 21 to 39.

## Methods

M1 is Multi-track. M2 is EXAONE 4.0 1.2B. M3 is EXAONE 3.5 2.4B Instruct. M4 is EXAONE 3.5 7.8B Instruct. Each method received 44 items, giving 880 ratings per method.
